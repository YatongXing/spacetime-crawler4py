Hi Everyone,

The TA/Reader application for Winter 2026 is now open! If you have a
fellowship that includes TA or Reader service for Winter quarter, you must
submit an application. You will not automatically be considered for a TA
position.

The TA/Reader application will be available online at:
https://tad.ics.uci.edu/

Requirements 

-Pass the English Proficiency Exam or be exempted
-Enrollment in 12 or more units
-Enrollment in 8 or more grad units per quarter
-Minimum 3.1 GPA or higher
-Cannot have grades lower than a C in the last 3 quarters
-Cannot have more than 2 "Incompletes"
-Cannot be on part time or filing fee status
-Must have attended TAPDP


Instructions
1.) In order to access the online application, you will need to log in
using your UCInetID. You must be connected to the campus VPN to submit an
application. Download the VPN from: https://oit.uci.edu/vpn
Will need to install the software client
Activate UCINetID and Duo to be able to access the VPN.

2.) Please complete both Step 1 and Step 2 of the application. Your
application will be considered incomplete if both steps are not completed.

> Please check the Searchable Schedule of Classes for a list of all CS,
ICS, Informatics, and Statistics undergraduate courses being offered this
Winter: https://www.reg.uci.edu/perl/WebSoc 


> When stating your course preferences, or courses you have TA'd,
please state the course number -- i.e. ICS 31, CS 161, etc. -- DO NOT
USE THE FIVE-DIGIT COURSE CODE. If you use the five-digit course code,
your application will not be considered.

>If this is your first quarter and you do not have a GPA, please put 0.0 in
the GPA box.

>Please see the English Proficiency policy here, and enter your score if

3.) Please be sure to SUBMIT in order to activate your application
