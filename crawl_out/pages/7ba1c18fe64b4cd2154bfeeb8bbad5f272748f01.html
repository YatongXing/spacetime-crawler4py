Subscriptions

email

Configure the format of your email notifications.

Add, remove or reorder subscription rules to email notifications. Only the first matching rule is applied.

Example: The rule "Never notify: I update a ticket" should be above "Notify: Any ticket changes" to get notifications of any ticket changes except when you update a ticket.

Note: See TracNotification for help on using notifications.
