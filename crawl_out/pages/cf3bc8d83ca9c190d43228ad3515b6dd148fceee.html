yunanc@ics.uci.edu 		Yunan Chen, PhD, FACMI

I am Professor of Informatics in the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences at the University of California, Irvine (UCI), with an adjunct appointment in the Joe C. Wen School of Population and Public Health, College of Health Sciences.

My area of research lies at the intersection of Human–Computer Interaction (HCI), Computer Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW), and Health Informatics. I am primarily interested in studying how health information is generated, managed, shared, and utilized to drive better healthcare in both clinical and patient-oriented settings.

Recent activities and news:

  • I currently serves as Interim Chair of the Department of Informatics
  • In February 2025, I was elected ACM Distinguished Member for my contributions to the socio-technical design of health technologies.
  • Since 2023, I have been serving as Consortium Chair for HCIC — my favorite conference venue in HCI
  • I was elected Fellow of the American College of Medical Informatics (FACMI) in 2023
  • I am part of UCI's Public Health Informatics & Technology Workforce Development Program (PHIT)
  • I was Chair of the Consumer Health Informatics Working Group of the American Medical Informatics Association (2023–2024)
  • From 2018 to 2024, I served as Vice Chair of Undergraduate Affairs in the the Department of Informatics
  • Check out my recent NSF grants on "Designing Health Data-Tracking Technologies for Children" and "Designing the Future of Risk Prediction IT in Fire Departments"
