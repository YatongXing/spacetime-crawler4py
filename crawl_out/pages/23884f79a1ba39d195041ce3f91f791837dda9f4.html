Data Science for Real-World Impact

Accelerate Your Data Science Career

Information Sessions

Data science is a driving force in today’s technological landscape, connecting our past to a future powered by intelligent systems.

Offered as a full-time or part-time program, the Master of Data Science (MDS) program is designed to cultivate expertise in contemporary data science methodologies. Our program emphasizes long-term value by equipping students with core statistical and computational skills that apply across sectors. Graduates have successfully transitioned into roles in technology, government, finance, and healthcare, often moving into advanced positions within two years. 

Who should apply?

Tech Professionals: Elevate your skillsets and experience to the next level and discover leadership opportunities.

Recent Graduates: Accelerate your data science career with a practical, hands-on curriculum and extensive networking opportunities that open doors to success in the field.

Career Changers: Stay relevant and competitive in a rapidly evolving tech landscape by diversifying your skillsets to meet the latest industry demands.

All applicants must have a bachelor’s degree and should have proficiency in at least two programming languages.

Career Development That Works for You

Exclusive to ICS Professional Master’s students, our dedicated Career Development team helps you transition into the industry and accelerate your career. From tailored coaching to proven job search strategies, we equip you with the skills, resources, and connections you need to stand out—during your program and long after graduation.

  • Receive career coaching
  • Develop job searching skills
  • Expand your network by connecting with industry professionals and alumni through events and programs
  • Engage with employers who are actively recruiting from UC Irvine
  • Access to job boards, career fairs, salary databases, and more

88% of graduates who reported employment engaged with the Career Development team through two primary tracked services—individual appointments and workshops.
