Patient Measurements

 all preoperative and postoperative patient measurements including: vitals, intake and output, pain levels, complications, and disposition

MRN

Medical record number which is a unique identifier for an individual patient.

LOG_ID

LOG_ID is the identification number for a single patient encounter.

FLO_NAME, FLO_DISPLAY_NAME

FLO_NAME is the category of measurement being taken and FLO_DISPLAY_NAME provides the name of the measurement being taken. 
 

MEAS_VALUE, UNITS

MEAS_VALUE is the value of the taken measurement and UNITS is the unit associated with the value. 
 

RECORD_TYPE, RECORD_TIME

RECORD_TYPE specifies when the the measurement was taken (pre, peri, or post operative) and RECORD_TIME specifies the exacts date and time at which the measurement was taken.
