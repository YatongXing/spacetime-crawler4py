Personal Details

I am an assistant professor in Department of Computer Science at New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), USA.
Check new website here.

Research interests: Databases, security, privacy, and IoT.

Students interested to work with me can see this.

I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow in Department of Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine, USA, working in the research group of Prof. Sharad Mehrotra.

I have completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, Beersheva, Israel.
I am blessed to become an academic son of Prof. Shlomi Dolev and to work with Prof. Jeffrey Ullman --- a Turing Award winner --- during my Ph.D. and Post-Doc .
