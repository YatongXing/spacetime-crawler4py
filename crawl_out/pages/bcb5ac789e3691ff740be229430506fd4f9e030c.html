    • Bio

    Chen Li is a professor in the Department of Computer Science at UC Irvine. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Stanford University, and his M.S. and B.S. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University, China, respectively.  His research interests are in the field of data management, including data-intensive computing, query processing and optimization, visualization, and text analytics. He was a recipient of an NSF CAREER award and several test-of-time publication awards, a part-time visiting research scientist at Google, PC co-chair of VLDB 2015, and an ACM distinguished member. He is the treasurer and a board member of the VLDB Endowment. He was a co-founder and CTO of a startup to commercialize his research results.
