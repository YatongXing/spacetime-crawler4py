The Portal Career Fair

10/14/2014 @ 3:30 pm - 7:00 pm

  • « Week 2: IOS Beginner Workshop
  • Stanford Class Series – Business Strategy and Monopoly Theory »

The Portal is hosting the first ever Tech Career Fair designed to connect talented and tech-savvy UCI students with over 20 of today’s new and innovative tech companies. Students who attend will have the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to network with the founders, engineers, and CTOs from these companies. Be sure to dress the part and bring multiple copies of your resume because you never know if you might secure an internship or job! These early-stage companies have many different positions that need to be filled, including data mining/coding, search engine optimization, marketing research and analysis, iOS and Android app development, and web design.

Registration will be at 3:30pm in front of Edwards Cinema, so please bring your UCI Student ID; after signing in, you will get a lanyard with your information and a QR code to be scanned by companies to increase your chances in the raffle at 6:45pm, where they will be giving away an iPad Mini (1st prize) and a Moto 360 smartwatch (2nd prize)!

Food will be provided from the local restaurants in the University Center for FREE at 6pm. If you haven’t sampled all the delicious food the University Center food court has to offer, here is your chance!

Registration will only allow the first 200 students access on a first come, first serve basis, so don’t miss out on this monumentous occassion!
