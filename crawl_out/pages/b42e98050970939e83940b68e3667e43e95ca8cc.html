About

Ian G. Harris is a Professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of California, Irvine. He is affiliated with the following research centers:

  • Center for Embedded and Cyber-physical Systems (CECS)
  • Cybersecurity Policy and Research Institute (CPRI)

Professor Harris received his BS in Computer Science from Massachusetts Institute of Technology, MS and PhD in Computer Science from the University of California San Diego. He conducts research in the areas of natural language processing and hardware security.

Research Areas

My research focuses on several key areas in natural language processing and cybersecurity:

Distributed Inference and Training of LLMs

Partitioning the training and inference processes across a set of resource-constrained IoT devices..

Security of LLMs

Detecting malicious prompts and mitigating their effects without disrupting helpfulness.

Hate and Toxic Speech Mitigation

Using LLMs to detect offensive speech and ensuring that LLMs do not generate offensive responses.

Embedded System Security

Designing and analyzing secure embedded system architectures.
