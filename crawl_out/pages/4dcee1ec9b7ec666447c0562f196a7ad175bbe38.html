Maintenance Notifications Summer 2020

Downtime Notice: ICS1 Server Room

  • Dates: 7/29/2020 - 8/2/2020
  • Duration:
    • Building power shut off at 0700 on 7/30/2020
    • Non-critical hosts shutdown beginning 7/29/2020
    • Some critical services may be unavailable for up to 8 hours.
    • It may take several days bring all research and instructional computing back online.

Affects:

ALL ICS SERVICES are affected

Reason:

The inspector for the fourth floor renovation project in ICS 1 informed us that a complete power down of the ICS building is necessary in order to install a new electrical panel in the 4th floor electrical room. The building's emergency backup generator will also need to be offline in order to perform this work.

This means that most, if not all, of the computing in the ICS Data Center will be powered down to accommodate this request. We will begin powering down non-critical services on 7/29/2020. Facilities will be providing a generator during this period keep some critical services up, but most equipment will be brought down prior to scheduled outage at 0700 on 7/30/2020. We will begin bringing all services back online immediately after work is complete. Priority will be placed on critical services, followed by services that impact the largest number of researchers and staff, and followed by instructional services. Although we intend to have all services restored as quickly as possible, some services may take longer than expected.

Shutdown Schedules

Hosts will be shutdown according to the following schedule beginning at 1300 (1pm) on 7/29/2020. Research NFS servers will be powered off beginning at 1700 (5pm) and disk arrays will be physically powered off at that time as well. Instructional hosts will be powered off beginning at 2200 (10pm).
