Google Team Drive

Free, unrestricted, self managed storage for shared data is available with the new Gsuite offering, Google Team Drive.

The drawbacks is that the space is not suitable for real time applications. It's a great place to keep documents for your research group and save archival data. We have some methods for quick and efficient transfer of data from you ICS home directory.

White Listing Addresses

The easiest way to whitelist an email address or prevent email from that account from going into your Gmail Spam folder is to add that email address to your Gmail Contact list. See this Google guide on using Contacts.

The other way to prevent an email message from going to Spam would be to set a filter for the email address not to be sent to the inbox. You can filter for a single email address or for an entire domain such as @uci.edu or @ics.uci.edu. See the following example.

Note: An additional filter is required in order to prevent your Sent items from appearing in your Inbox when filtering the entire domain you are sending from.

Create a Gmail filter to White List UCI Addresses

These step by step directions create a filter that effectively whitelists any email from an UCI email address.

  1. Open Gmail
  2. Go to the gear symbol (upper right)
  3. Select Settings
  4. Click on Filters and Blocked Addresses
  5. Click on Create a new filter
  6. In the From field, type: *uci.edu
  7. Click on Create filter with this search »
  8. Check Never send it to Spam
  9. Click on the Create filter button

Now, everything sent by and received from UCI email addresses will show up in your inbox, including messages sent by you as you create them or reply to others. To archive your sent messages, repeat the steps above to create a filter with the following conditions:

  1. From field: yourusername@ics.uci.edu
  2. Action to check: Skip the Inbox (Archive it)

Undergraduate ICS Gsuite Accounts

Ungraduate ICS Gsuite accounts are created on a case by case basis strictly for working with GCP projects. Project owners may send email to helpdesk@ics.uci.edu in order to have an undergraduate student included in the ugrad_gsuite group.

Troubleshooting

Turn off login challenge

In some situations, an authorized user can’t verify their identity. If this happens, as an administrator you can temporarily turn off the login challenge to allow them to sign in. To temporarily disable the Login Challenge:

  1. Go to Users List.
  2. In the Users List, find the user and click their name to display the user information page.
  3. Click Security.
  4. Click Login challenge.
  5. Click Turn Off For 10 Minutes.

Note: It might take several minutes for this change to take effect on the user account. At that point, the login challenge is off for 10 minutes to allow the user to sign in.
