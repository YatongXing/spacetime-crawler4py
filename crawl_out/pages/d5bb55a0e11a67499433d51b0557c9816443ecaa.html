Grid Engine 8.1.9 Queue Updates 12/18/2017

Beginning on December 18th, the rest of our Grid Engine queus will be migrated to version 8.1.9.

How does this affect you:

Users must ensure that all scheduled jobs have finshed before the move on 12/18/2017 at 0800. If the jobs have not completed, then users will need to resubmit their jobs to the new queue.

  • Current SGE configurations has been copied direction to the new servers.

  • Jobs running before migration will not longer be visible after the transition, although some may complete.

Test out the new service: Login to andromeda-1 and submit jobs to the openlab.q. Notify helpdesk@ics.uci.edu if you encounter any problems.

Affects

OIT Network and Security Engineers will be enhancing the campus Border Router and Border Firewall bandwidth on Dec 5th between 2AM - 5AM.

Computer Science Building Network Upgrade 11-4-2017

  • SYSTEM: Computer Science Building 302 Network Routers
  • DATE: 11/4
  • START TIME: 0600
  • DURATION: 2 hours

(Please note this is an estimate; systems may be up sooner or later than expected, due to unforeseeable circumstances.)

AFFECTS:

The Computer Science Building 302 and Information Computer Science 2 Building 304 will lose connectivity to campus during this period.

The ICS data center has a redundant 10Gb/s links to the campus backbone and should experience only a brief outage, if any, while spanning tree re-establishes. However, this is the first production test of fail over and care should be taken if you are conducting important research into or out of the ICS Data Center during this period.

REASON:

Campus network engineers from OIT will be swapping components that are due to be EOL'd this coming January out of the Cisco router on the first floor of the building.

Note From OIT 

OIT's network engineers will be upgrading the building router at ICS (302) on Saturday, November 4 at 6 AM. ICS2 (304) is also connected to this building router, so this building will also be affected. Any UCI-Lightpath connections will not be affected.

START: 6 AM, Sat 11/4/17
END:  8 AM, Sat 11/4/17
SCOPE: Replacing unsupported network modules inside the building router's chassis.

COMMENTS:
All network connectivity to and from the building router will not be available during the outage. The upgrade should take approximately 30 minutes, but we set aside 2 hours to troubleshoot any unforeseen issues.
