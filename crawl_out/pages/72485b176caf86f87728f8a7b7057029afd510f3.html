WISEN will be held at the University of California, Irvine (UCI). UCI is 10 minutes/4 miles from the Orange County/John Wayne Airport. It is 1 hour - 1 hour 30 minutes (depending on traffic)/45 miles from LAX.

The workshop will be held in the McDonnell Douglas Auditorium. Breakout sessions will be held in the new Information and Computer Science building (ICS2), rooms 136 and 144.

Directions and printable maps of UCI (including the McDonnell Douglas Auditorium, building #311, and ICS2, building #304) and the surrounding area are available. A map and parking information will be mailed to you upon registration.
