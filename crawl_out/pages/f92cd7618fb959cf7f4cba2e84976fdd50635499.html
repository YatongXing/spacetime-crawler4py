Patient Visit

 diagnosis and diagnosis code for a particular visit

MRN

Medical record number which is a unique identifier for an individual patient. MRNs will appear in multiple rows of this table, signifying that this patient has multiple diagnoses listed in their EMR.

LOG_ID

LOG_ID is the identification number for a single patient encounter.

diagnosis_code, dx_name

diagnosis_code is the International Classification of Diseases, Ninth Revision, Clinical Modification (ICD-9-CM) codes for each patient diagnosis available in their EMR and dx_name is the name of the corresponding diagnosis
